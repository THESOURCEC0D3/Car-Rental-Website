About Fleetly

Connecting car owners with renters across Nigeria

Our Story

Founded in 2025, fleetly was born from a single 0bservation: many car owners have vehicles sitting idle while many people need short-term transportation solutions.

We set out to bridge the gap by making by making a pltform that makes car rental as easy as booking a ride.

Today we are proud to serve thousands of users across Nigeria. Facilitating tens o thousands of successful rentals and helping car owners earn extra income.

Our Mission

To revolusionize car rental in Nigeria by creating a trusted platform that empowers car owners to monetize their vehicles, and providesrenetrs with affordable, convenient access to quality transportation.

We believe in the sharing economy and the power of technology to connect people, reduce costs, and maximize the utilization of exisiting resources.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform for car rental in Nigeria, connecting car owners and renters seamlessly through a user-friendly, secure, and efficient service.
